About


Welcome to my project website.

I enjoy harvesting information from remote sensing and social-economic datasets and eventually building models that explain and predict.

Most projects I have done so far combined geospatial data and climate models, and focused on the climate feedback of landcover changes. Through these projects, I have acquired experiences working with Linux clusters, parallel computing, machine learning and model optimization. I use Python as my everyday tool, and also work with Matlab and R. I am looking forward to expanding my expertise to different type of datasets and models.

I currently hold a B.S. in Ecology from Peking University and M.S. in Earth System Science from Univeristy of California Irvine. I also enjoy playing guitar and cello and doing photography when I have a chance.
